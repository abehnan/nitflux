Welcome To NitFlux!
A space to independently review Netflix content!
Who are we?
A trio of McGill University students who consume obscene amounts of Netflix movies, and television.
Why are you doing this?
In early 2017 NetFlix decided to dump its star based rating scheme for a simple would or would not reccomend. This lead to a complete overhaul in not only the reccomendation algorithmns, but also how users chose which shows or movies to watch. Here we wish to provide a space to Netflix lovers to rate review content independtly from nextlix and in the previous style.
